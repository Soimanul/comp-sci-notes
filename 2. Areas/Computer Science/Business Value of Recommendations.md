## Definition

The **business value of recommendation systems** is the measurable impact on revenue, engagement, and growth generated by deploying personalised recommendation algorithms at scale.

> [!info] Key Intuition
> Recommendations are not a "nice to have" feature — they are often **the single largest revenue driver** in digital businesses, because they convert passive browsers into active buyers by surfacing exactly what they want.

## Industry Evidence

| Company | Metric | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Amazon** | 35% of revenue from recommendations | McKinsey |
| **Netflix** | 75% of viewing comes from recommendations | McKinsey |

## Speed to Value

An end-to-end recommendation solution that previously required **9 person-months** was delivered in **3 weeks** using the open-source `recommenders` library.

> [!example]- Estimation
> Amazon's 35% revenue attribution: ~$23 billion in 2013, estimated ~$130 billion in 2024 as overall Amazon revenue has grown dramatically.

## Significance

These numbers explain why recommendation systems consistently deliver the **highest ROI** of any AI investment. A 1% improvement in recommendation quality can translate to hundreds of millions in revenue at scale.
