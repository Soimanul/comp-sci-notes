## Definition
Depth of field is the range of object distances that appear sufficiently sharp.

## Core Ideas
- Large aperture → shallow depth of field
- Small aperture → large depth of field

## Mathematical Criterion
Depth of field includes all distances for which:

$$
b < c
$$

(where symbols are as above)
