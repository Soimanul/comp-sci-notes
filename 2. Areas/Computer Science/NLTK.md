## Definition
NLTK is a Python library oriented around classical, linguistically motivated NLP, exposing many core building blocks (tokenization, tagging, parsing, corpora access) as explicit components.

## What it is used for
- Learning and experimenting with traditional NLP techniques.
- Accessing corpora and linguistic resources.
- Implementing or inspecting pipeline steps manually.

## Strengths
- High transparency and educational value.
- Broad coverage of classical NLP tasks and resources.

## Limitations
- Less optimized for production-scale pipelines compared to newer libraries.
- Many workflows require more manual assembly and configuration.
