> [!abstract]- TL;DR
> Recommendation systems are evaluated using offline metrics (rating and ranking metrics computed during development) and online metrics (business KPIs measured in production). Offline metrics include RMSE, MAE, Precision@K, Recall@K, NDCG@K, and MAP@K. Online/business metrics include CTR, Conversion Rate, LTV, and MAU. A/B testing bridges the two.

## Overview

Knowing which model is "best" requires the right evaluation framework. Most models optimise for offline ranking metrics, but ultimate success is measured by business outcomes in production.
