## Definition
Exposure is the total amount of light collected by the sensor.

## Control Factors
1. **[[Aperture]] (Area):** Size of the lens opening. Controls **Depth of Field**.
    
2. **Shutter Speed (Time):** Duration the sensor is active. Controls **Motion Blur**.
    
3. **ISO (Gain):** Sensitivity of the sensor. Controls **Noise**.
