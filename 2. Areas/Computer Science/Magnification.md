## Definition
The ratio between the size of the object in the image ($x$) and its actual size in the world ($X$).

## Formula
$$m = \frac{x}{X} = -\frac{f}{Z}$$

- $f$: Focal length.
    
- $Z$: Distance of the object from the pinhole.
    
- **Negative Sign:** Indicates the image is inverted.
