## Definition
Photometric image formation deals with the transformation of light energy into pixel values. It answers: "How bright is this pixel?"

## Core Dependencies
1. **Radiance:** The amount of light emitted/reflected by the scene surface. (Depends on lights and material).
    
2. **Irradiance:** The amount of light falling on the camera sensor. (Depends on [[Aperture]] and lens properties).
    
3. **[[Exposure]]:** The integration of light over time.
