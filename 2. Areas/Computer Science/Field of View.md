## Definition

The geometric extent of the scene that is visible to the camera. It is determined by the **Focal Length (**$f$**)** and the **Sensor Size (**$h$**)**.

## Mathematical Foundation
$$\text{FOV} (\alpha) = 2 \cdot \arctan\left(\frac{h}{2f}\right)$$

- **Wide Angle (Short** $f$**):** Large FOV, capturing more of the scene.
    
- **Telephoto (Long** $f$**):** Small FOV, capturing a narrow slice (zoomed in).
