## Definition
The variable opening in the lens that limits the amount of light entering the camera.

## Notation
Measured in **f-stops** ($N = f/D$).

- **Small N (e.g., f/1.8):** Wide opening. More light. Shallow DOF.
    
- **Large N (e.g., f/22):** Narrow opening. Less light. Deep DOF.
