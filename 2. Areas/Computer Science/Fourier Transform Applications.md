## Overview
This hub covers the practical applications of the 2D Fourier transform in image processing: frequency-domain filtering (low-pass, high-pass, band-pass), the convolution theorem that links spatial and frequency domains, image shifting and resizing in Fourier space, deconvolution for image restoration, and the Nyquist sampling theorem.
