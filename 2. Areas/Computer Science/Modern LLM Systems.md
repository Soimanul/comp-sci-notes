> [!abstract]- TL;DR
> Modern AI systems have evolved from rule-based finite state machines (Software 1.0) through probabilistic models (Software 2.0) to LLM-based systems where context is state. The three key LLM application patterns are RAG (retrieval-augmented generation), Agentic Workflows (function calling, ReAct), and Evaluation + Guardrails.

## Overview

This session bridges recommendation systems and modern LLM-based chatbots. Understanding the evolution clarifies *why* LLMs replaced rule-based bots and *how* they are deployed in production.
