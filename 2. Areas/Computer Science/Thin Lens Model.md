## Definition
The thin lens model approximates a real lens as a single refracting element.

## Core Ideas
- Rays from a scene point converge to form a focused image point

## Mathematical Formulation
The thin lens equation states:

$$
\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{d_o} + \frac{1}{d_i}
$$

Where:
- $f$: focal length
- $d_o$: object distance
- $d_i$: image distance
