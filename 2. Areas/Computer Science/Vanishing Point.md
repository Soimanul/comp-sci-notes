## Definition
The point on the image plane where projections of parallel lines in 3D space intersect.

## Core Idea
In [[Perspective Projection]], parallel lines are not preserved (unlike in [[Orthographic Projection]]). As they recede into the distance ($Z \to \infty$), their projection converges to a specific coordinate $(x, y)$.

- **Horizon:** The line formed by the vanishing points of all horizontal lines in the scene.
