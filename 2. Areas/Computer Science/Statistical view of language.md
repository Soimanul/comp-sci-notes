## Definition
Language is treated as observable data, and linguistic structure and meaning are inferred statistically from patterns of usage rather than specified as explicit rules.

## What it focuses on
- **Distribution:** how words and constructions occur across contexts.
- **Frequency:** how often patterns appear in real text.
- **Regularities of use:** co-occurrence and statistical association as evidence for structure and meaning.

## Core method
- Start from corpora and compute representations or models from observed counts, co-occurrences, and learned parameters.
- Evaluate models empirically using predictive performance or retrieval quality rather than grammatical derivability.
