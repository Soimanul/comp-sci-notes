## Definition
Image formation is the process by which a three-dimensional scene is mapped onto a two-dimensional image plane.

## Core Ideas
- Cameras map 3D world points to 2D image points
- Image formation has two complementary aspects:
  - **Geometric:** where a point appears in the image
  - **Photometric:** how bright that point appears
