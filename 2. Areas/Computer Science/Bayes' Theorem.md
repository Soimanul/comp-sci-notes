## Definition
Bayes' formula (or Bayes' Theorem) is a mathematical formula used to determine the conditional probability of an event based on prior knowledge of conditions that might be related to the event.

## Core Mechanics
The formula is expressed as:

$$ P(A|B) = P(A) \frac{P(B|A)}{P(B)} $$

In the context of NLP classification:
- $P(Class|Document)$ is the **Posterior** probability (what we want to find).
- $P(Class)$ is the **Prior** probability (how common the class is).
- $P(Document|Class)$ is the **Likelihood** (how likely the document is given the class).
- $P(Document)$ is the **Evidence** (marginal probability of the document).

## Significance
Bayes' formula allows us to reverse conditional probabilities, enabling us to estimate the probability of a class given a set of features (words) by modeling how those features are generated by each class.
