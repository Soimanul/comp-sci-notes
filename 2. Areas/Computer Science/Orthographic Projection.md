## Definition
A simplified projection model where light rays are **parallel** to the optical axis, rather than converging to a center.

## Conditions for Use
It is a valid approximation when the object is very far away, or the variation in depth ($\Delta Z$) is negligible compared to the absolute depth ($Z$).

- i.e., $Z \gg \Delta Z$.
    

## Formula
$$x = c \cdot X$$$$y = c \cdot Y$$

(Where $c$ is a constant scaling factor). The image size does **not** change as the object moves closer or further.
