## Definition
A CFG derivation example demonstrates how a sentence is generated by expanding the start symbol step-by-step until only terminals remain, making the underlying constituent structure explicit.

## What the example illustrates
- Derivation proceeds by applying production rules to expand non-terminals into constituents.
- The same sentence can be represented as:
  - a sequence of rewrite steps (derivation), and
  - a hierarchical parse tree (structural description).

## Why it matters
It shows how CFGs formalize grammatical structure, but also motivates later questions about meaning, since a valid parse does not automatically resolve semantic interpretation.
