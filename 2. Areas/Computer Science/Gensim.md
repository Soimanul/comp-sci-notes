## Definition
Gensim is a Python library focused on vector-space and topic modeling methods, providing scalable implementations of representations and models such as BoW, TF–IDF, LSA, and LDA.

## What it is used for
- Building sparse and latent semantic representations from text.
- Topic modeling and document similarity pipelines.
- Large-scale semantic modeling with emphasis on efficiency.

## Strengths
- Strong support for classical distributional and latent semantic methods.
- Scales well for corpus-level modeling.

## Limitations
- Not designed as a full general NLP pipeline (less emphasis on syntax and token-level linguistic annotation).
- Typically used alongside other libraries for preprocessing and linguistic features.
