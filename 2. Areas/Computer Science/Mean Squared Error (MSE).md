## Definition
A loss function used primarily for **Regression** tasks. It measures the average of the squares of the errors (the difference between predicted and actual values).

## Formula
$$MSE = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2$$

## Significance
Squaring the error ensures the result is always positive and penalizes larger errors more heavily than smaller ones.
