> [!abstract]- TL;DR
> Recommendation systems are AI solutions with the highest ROI in industry. They personalize user experience at scale, driving 35% of Amazon's revenue, 75% of Netflix viewing, and 90% of TikTok content. Building one requires understanding the user-item interaction model and the wide variety of industry use cases.

## Overview

Recommendation systems (recos) solve the problem of **meeting unmet, unarticulated customer needs at scale**. Instead of showing all users the same products, a reco behaves like a world-class salesperson — finding the right product for the right user at the right time.
