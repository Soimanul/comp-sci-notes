> [!abstract]- TL;DR
> Transfer learning in NLP applies a model pre-trained on a large corpus to a downstream task with minimal additional training. BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) pre-trains a Transformer encoder using masked language modelling (MLM) and next sentence prediction (NSP), then fine-tunes on specific tasks. Zero-shot learning applies a model to tasks it was never explicitly fine-tuned on.

## Overview

Pre-training on large text corpora and fine-tuning on small task-specific datasets unlocked state-of-the-art performance across almost every NLP benchmark. This session explains why and how.
