> [!abstract]- TL;DR
> SAR (Smart Adaptive Recommendations) is a memory-based item-item collaborative filtering algorithm. It computes user-item affinities and item-item similarities to score recommendations. Matrix Factorization (SVD, ALS) is the canonical model-based CF approach, learning latent factor representations for users and items.

## Overview

This session covers two foundational collaborative filtering approaches in depth: the memory-based SAR algorithm and the model-based Matrix Factorization family (SVD and ALS).
